Objective:

Create a system that promotes participation and builds relationships.

Once a week (Perhaps every Sunday), The Top 20 Heroes of the week will be posted in #general

Top 20 Heroes of the Week Points Structure:

1st: 100 pts
2nd - 5th: 75 pts
5th - 10th: 60pts
10th - 20th: 45 pts
Active for the week : 20 pts


These points will apply to the yearly overall rankings. At the end of the year, awards can be given to top performers.

Badges:
Hero Overall (Current Overall leader)
Hero of the Week
Top 10 Hero Overall (Current year)
Top 20 Hero Overall (Current year)
